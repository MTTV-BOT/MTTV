# MTTV Bot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MTTV Bot collects, stores, and uses information necessary to operate the Bot.

## 2. Information Collected

The Bot may collect and store:

- Discord User IDs
- Discord Server IDs
- Discord Channel IDs
- Vehicle inventory information
- Trade history
- Game progression data

The Bot does NOT collect:

- Passwords
- Email addresses
- Payment information
- Private Discord messages unless required by a command

## 3. Purpose of Data Collection

Data is used solely to:

- Maintain inventories
- Process trades
- Save user progress
- Operate Bot features
- Prevent abuse and exploits

## 4. Data Storage

Data may be stored on secure servers, databases, or hosting platforms used by the Bot.

## 5. Data Sharing

MTTV Bot does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties except when required by law.

## 6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for operation of the Bot.

## 7. User Rights

Users may request:

- Information about stored data
- Correction of stored data
- Deletion of stored data where technically possible

## 8. Security

Reasonable security measures are used to protect stored information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is intended only for users who meet Discord's minimum age requirements.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.
